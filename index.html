Welcome to GatorShop

Abstract

A group of UHD students have been developing a website that different people can shop from called GatorShop. In this project, we mainly use HTML, CSS and Javascript to build our website. The website offers around 10 different items that are necessary and useful to college students in general . For each item, we include the information about the item and may include a link to purchase.
